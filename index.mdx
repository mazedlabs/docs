Mazed is a multimodal AI agent platform. Design agents visually, connect them to your knowledge and tools, and deploy them to phone, web, or video — no code required.

<Note>
  The documentation is under construction. Full guides for building, integrating, and deploying agents are coming soon.
</Note>

## What you'll find here

When the docs launch, they will cover the full Mazed workflow:

- **Design your agent** — build conversation flows on the Agent Canvas, connect knowledge bases, and define behaviors.
- **Integrate your stack** — plug into your CRM, helpdesk, calendar, and 50+ tools with pre-built connectors.
- **Go live** — deploy to telephony, web, or embedded in your product, across voice, video, and text.

You'll also find references for platform capabilities like persistent memory, native actions, multi-agent orchestration, and human handoff.
